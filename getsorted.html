What is GetSorted?

GetSorted is a home inventory and organization app designed to help you organize your home by Spaces, Containers, and Items. Whether you're managing a kitchen pantry, a storage unit, or an entire home, GetSorted helps you know exactly what you own and where it lives.

Flagship Feature: Organize From Photo

GetSorted's flagship feature uses AI to help you organize items from a simple photo. Photograph a shelf, drawer, closet, or box — and GetSorted will identify likely items and suggest how to organize them. You always review and approve suggestions before anything is saved.

Planned Features

  • Spaces, Containers, and Items hierarchy
  • AI-assisted photo organization
  • Search across your entire inventory
  • Smart Labels and QR code support (planned)
  • Moving Mode (planned)
  • Insurance and estate planning exports (planned)
  • Household sharing (planned)
