Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

BrightCasa LLC ("BrightCasa," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit brightcasa.co (the "Website"), contact us, or choose to receive communications from us.

This Privacy Policy applies to the BrightCasa website only. BrightCasa products and mobile apps, including GetSorted and SpendBright, may have separate privacy policies before they are publicly released.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide to us, such as:

  • Your name

  • Information you provide when requesting updates, support, or other communications

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Website, our hosting provider and related services may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Pages viewed

This information is generally used to help operate, secure, maintain, and improve the Website.

2. How We Use Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to messages, questions, or support requests
  • Communicate about BrightCasa, our products, updates, or services
  • Maintain and improve the Website
  • Understand how visitors use the Website
  • Protect the Website from spam, fraud, misuse, or security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights

We will not use your personal information for purposes that are materially different from those described in this Privacy Policy without providing notice where required by law.

If you choose to receive emails from BrightCasa, we may send product updates, announcements, or other relevant communications.

BrightCasa does not sell your personal information.

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including responding to requests, maintaining records, meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements.

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us, subject to applicable law and verification of your identity.

To make a privacy-related request, email hello@brightcasa.co.
