SpendBright

Spend with confidence. Save with purpose.

SpendBright is currently in early planning and development. It is not yet publicly available.

What is SpendBright?

SpendBright is a practical spending-confidence app designed to help you understand what you can truly afford to spend — after your upcoming bills, income, savings goals, and planned expenses are considered.

The Safe to Spend Metric

SpendBright's core feature is Safe to Spend — a single, honest number that shows your real discretionary budget after your financial commitments are accounted for. No guesswork. No surprises.

Planned Features

  • Safe to Spend metric
  • Upcoming bills and income tracking
  • Protected savings goals
  • Planned spending categories
  • Clear, explainable financial summaries
  • Privacy-first design

Privacy

SpendBright is designed with privacy in mind. Financial data is sensitive — BrightCasa will never sell your data.
